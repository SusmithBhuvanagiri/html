• Verizon Communications Inc. is a major American telecommunications company that provides a wide range of services including wireless communications,
    internet services, and digital television. It is one of the largest telecom companies in the United States and operates under the Verizon brand.

    Founded in 1983 as Bell Atlantic, the company rebranded to Verizon in 2000 following a merger with GTE. Verizon is known for its extensive wireless network,
    which covers a significant portion of the U.S. population, and it offers various plans for mobile phones, tablets, and other devices.

    In addition to its wireless services, Verizon also provides broadband internet through its Fios service
    , which offers high-speed fiber-optic internet and television services. The company has also made significant investments in 5G technology, aiming to enhance connectivity and support the growing demand for data.
